Driver question

How do I safely recover a car on the highway shoulder?

TARA Response

Immediate Risk: Exposure to high-speed traffic and limited escape space.

First Action: Position the tow truck as a barrier, activate warning lights, and wear high-visibility PPE.

Safe Procedure: Approach from the safest side, deploy warning devices, secure the vehicle, and minimize time on scene.

Final Warning: Expect distracted drivers and stop work if the scene becomes unsafe. Follow company policy and local regulations.
